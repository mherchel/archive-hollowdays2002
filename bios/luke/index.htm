In 1935 the german think tank manager Jorge Williams proficized that in the early 1980's a child would be born. The child would be born with the abilitly to keep time and rythem in an uncanny way. Before the child was even born, his mother patted her belly to the rythem and beat to the great music of the time, David Boiwe, The Who, The Police, The Loving Spoonfull, Cyndi Lauper, underground bootlegged Mettallica no life till leather tapes, And a many assortment of random artists. Little did she know that she would be molding the profiscied one to be the greatest drummer ever.Under the cleaving moons of BA and Uri the child was born. When he was 4 he was given an indian totum drum.The child soon broke it. When he was 12 and in public school he was given the choice of playing tuba or the drums, the choice being predetermined. He chose the snare drum. With the cards on the table and the motions
set into place the formation of a rawk god begain. Lessons started and a man by the name of Jaret started molding and forming pure raw energy into the well oiled refined playing machine that he is today. Starting with the basic rudementry beats and pentameter of Pearl Jam and Alice in Chains, and finnaly graduating to the metal crunch of Metallica. From then on the boy was being formed into what he is today. For about 2 yrs. the boy lost all intrest in his gifts. He spent his time honing the dark majiks of sothern folklore. It was a move that once again jump started the prodigy into drums. From there he started playing from morning to night, a couple of time the law was called to make him stop. Little did they know that it just motivated him to play harder , louder and faster. After locking himself into his room for 3 yrs he felt it was time to suprise the world with his playing ablities. According to urbin legend he roams around playing genra to genra, bestowing his gifts and talents to all who wish to a part of them. He gives freely to those in need. Administers justice to those who lack justice. He brings light to dark and night to day. the hunter of the shadow is rising. He is here to stay to bring the love of music to all who wish to hear.
He is I and I am him, together we join to form me.
