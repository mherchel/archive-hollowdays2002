Jeremy
Hate, death, revolt, love, chaos, longing, are among the many different emotions that Nick Estes writes about and trys to convey to an audience. Commanding the crowd, inviting them in, and making them feel the lyrics is exactly what the frontman insists upon.

Born July 3rd, 1980, in Swartz Creek, MI., Nick moved to Ft. Myers, Fl. at age 10. Moving to Gainesville 3 years ago, Nick found himself in the middle of a very promising music scene.

"Finding that perfect band, that can bring out the music that is in my head, is a hard thing to do. Yet that is exactly what I've found." he says. Having found the group with the right tools to do the job, Nick hopes for one thing,
"I want to be able to reach a lot of people with what we are doing. I mean it's not everyday someone comes along to try and save rock and roll. Music has definitely gotten off track, we are just here to kick it's ass back into gear, and bring music back to what music should be, meaningful."

Nick is influenced by bands like The Doors, Pink Floyd, Led Zepplin, Nirvana, The Who, and Lynyrd Skynyrd, just to name a few.
