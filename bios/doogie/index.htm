Leslie N. "Doogie" Wells
     
  i stand in darkness in search of light,
in need of peace, inside of fright.
Behind a wall of broken stone,
i stand in silence; i stand alone.
  my wind blows from day to day;
feelings come and pass away.
my eyes are lit, but lit no more,
by dark things stirring on the shore.
  In fear i flee to other plains,
to forget my never ending pain,
to break the bars of life-long lies,
to escape the dark that burns my eyes.
  I break the surface of blackest shade
free from the hell My heart has made.
My grave lies empty in the sun;
My new life has, at last, begun.
