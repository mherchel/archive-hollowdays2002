Joe

What's up? This is Joe, thanks for checking my bio. First thing I wanna say is how cool it is to be in a kick ass band again. This is what I was born to do and I can't wait to hit the stage. Now for some boring facts: Born in Pennsylvania on Jan 17, 1978 (my mom says my first words were "Make some Fuckin' Noise, Gainesville!" but I don't believe her) moved to Hobe Sound, Fla when I was 12, now I'm straight outta Compton. Anyway I've been playing guitar for a long time now, influenced and inspired by guys like Randy Rhoads, Zakk Wylde, Slash, Jimmy Page... you get the idea. Like most of those guys, I sling a Les Paul, and as great as it is, it's heavy! In fact my left shoulder bone has actually grown with a big curve in it where my guitar strap sets. That's when you know you're hardcore, when your bones grow around your instrument!
